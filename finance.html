Personal Savings Bank Account

Allows zero balance account opening facility. Ideal for earning regular interest on your deposits.

Fixed Deposit Scheme

A long-term savings plan where the account holder earns an attractive annual interest on the principal which he/she would have deposited as a bulk amount.

Nithya Nidhi

A day-to-day savings bank account, aimed at micro and small-scale traders and market vendors of agro-products who need safety, security and growth of their daily cash earnings

Monthly Deposit Scheme (MDS)

Up to 80% of the deposit amount can be availed of, as a loan for agricultural purposes by the account holder

Micro Entrepreneurial Development Plan

A convenient funding scheme for marginal farmers and micro entrepreneurs to develop their livelihood

Business Loan

Aimed at startup entrepreneurs and established agro-business owners. Covers working capital, expansion plans, new investments etc.

Why State Agri Horti Society?

Indias planned approach to self-sufficiency in agriculture and improved rural economy gained pace with the green revolution of the 70s. During the decades that followed, several developmental initiatives made in the agro-industrial and economic infrastructure built the co-operative agri-horticulture sector to become one of the countrys largest spread of national resources, even by global dimensions. It is against this dynamic growth background that the Society earned its trust and goodwill to become a 2000-member farmers organization that continues to improve the lives of South Keralas rural population.
