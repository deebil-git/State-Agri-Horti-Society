Services

Agriculture

Agro-processing, value addition, incubation centre, start-up program, agri-tourism, farm tourism, state and national programs, seeds and planting materials, nursery management and training, soft and hard-skill development, agri clinic and disease management, agri business promotion, other areas.

Horticulture

Landscaping, arboriculture and floriculture support, land mapping, preparation, development and management of gardens, bio-diversity parks and horticultural surroundings, development of social forestry, etc.

Irrigation

Groundwater development and management, water quality testing and assurance, micro-irrigation projects, rain and natural water harvesting, waste water management, aquaponics, etc

Dairy, Poultry & Animal Husbandry

Training and technology transfer for commercial dairy, poultry, apiary, piggery and other livestock farm development and management, community farm produce marketing assistance, veterinary clinical service support, animal feeds, nutrition and disease management services, breeding stock procurement assistance and other related services.

Aquaculture

Inland fresh water fish farm development and management assistance, commercial and ornamental aquaculture, aquaponics and aeroponics,

Environment Preservation
& Eco-Tourism

Ecology and environment preservation, agri-tourism, farm tourism, green belt and social forestry development, tribal villages and crafts preservation, water bodies and natural aqua-source conservation, popularisation of organic farming, green leafy vegetables, neutraceuticals, among others.

Project Report & Management

Preparation of agri-horticultural and related project reports (DPR), feasibility studies, monitoring, evaluation and impact assessment studies, alternate energy management system developments, etc.

Statutory Audits

Lab and data analyses, standards and statutory assessments, public health inspection and hygiene audits of hotels, clubs, restaurants and similar food preparation units as assigned by corporation and municipal authorities, approval of quality procedures and control for certifications, sanitation and water quality assessment for rural households, training of manpower and process systems for international standards and certification, regulatory control and monitoring assignments across the sectors.

Training & Workshops

Special academic and field application-oriented training programs for the farming community are conducted on regular basis. Seminars and Workshops are also announced periodically to update information base and skills development. The Society also participates at State, National and International level Conferences and Workshops for garnering new technologies and innovations in the areas of Soil Testing, Climate Change, Water Management, Geographical Evaluation and Environmental Impact, Agro-Economics, Project Management, Start-up Incubation, etc.

Why State Agri Horti Society?

Indias planned approach to self-sufficiency in agriculture and improved rural economy gained pace with the green revolution of the 70s. During the decades that followed, several developmental initiatives made in the agro-industrial and economic infrastructure built the co-operative agri-horticulture sector to become one of the countrys largest spread of national resources, even by global dimensions. It is against this dynamic growth background that the Society earned its trust and goodwill to become a 2000-member farmers organization that continues to improve the lives of South Keralas rural population.
