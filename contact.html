Why State Agri Horti Society?

Indias planned approach to self-sufficiency in agriculture and improved rural economy gained pace with the green revolution of the 70s. During the decades that followed, several developmental initiatives made in the agro-industrial and economic infrastructure built the co-operative agri-horticulture sector to become one of the countrys largest spread of national resources, even by global dimensions.
